Our Services

1. Dog Training Programs

Professional training to help your rescue dog adjust to their new home.

Programs Offered:

  • Basic obedience training - Mondays & Wednesdays, 6pm - 7pm
  • House training assistance - Tuesdays, 5pm - 6pm
  • Socialization classes - Saturdays, 10am - 11am
  • Behavioral modification - By appointment, Monday-Friday
Learn More

2. Post-Adoption Support

We're here for you after adoption with ongoing support.

Support Services:

  • 24/7 emergency hotline - Available anytime
  • Follow-up calls during first month - Weeks 1, 2, and 4
  • Behavioral consultation - Thursdays, 2pm - 5pm
  • Re-homing assistance if needed - By appointment
Contact Support

3. Behavioral Consultation

Certified behaviorists help with challenging issues.

Issues We Address:

  • Separation anxiety - Consultation sessions every Tuesday, 3pm - 5pm
  • Fear and aggression - Wednesday sessions, 1pm - 4pm
  • Excessive barking - Friday workshops, 11am - 12pm
  • Integration with other pets - Saturday sessions, 2pm - 4pm
Schedule Consultation

4. Community Education Programs

Workshops and seminars on responsible pet ownership.

Workshop Schedule:

  • New Dog Owner 101 - First Saturday of month, 9am - 11am
  • Understanding Dog Behavior - Second Thursday, 6pm - 8pm
  • Dog Health and Wellness - Third Wednesday, 7pm - 9pm
  • Advanced Training Techniques - Last Friday, 5pm - 7pm
View Workshops

5. Veterinary Care Partnerships

Local veterinarian partnerships for your pet's health.

Partnership Benefits:

  • Discounted initial vet visits - Available at 5 partner clinics
  • Referrals to trusted clinics - Monday-Friday, 9am - 5pm
  • Low-cost spay/neuter services - First Tuesday of month, 8am - 2pm
  • Emergency care network - 24/7 emergency referrals
