Our Services

Paws & Hearts offers a full range of support services to help adopters and their rescue dogs thrive together. Browse our service categories below to find the program that fits your needs and check the schedule for each session.

  1. Dog Training Programs

    Professional training to help your rescue dog adjust to their new home and learn essential skills.

    Hours of Operation:

    • Basic obedience training — Mondays & Wednesdays, 6:00pm – 7:00pm
    • House training assistance — Tuesdays, 5:00pm – 6:00pm
    • Socialization classes — Saturdays, 10:00am – 11:00am
    • Behavioral modification — By appointment, Monday – Friday
    Learn More

  2. Post-Adoption Support

    We’re here for you after adoption with ongoing support to make the transition smooth for both you and your new companion.

    Hours of Operation:

    • 24/7 emergency hotline — Available anytime, day or night
    • Follow-up calls — Weeks 1, 2, and 4 after adoption
    • Behavioral consultation — Thursdays, 2:00pm – 5:00pm
    • Re-homing assistance — By appointment, Monday – Friday
    Contact Support

  3. Behavioral Consultation

    Certified behaviorists help with challenging issues so your dog can feel safe, calm, and confident at home.

    Hours of Operation:

    • Separation anxiety sessions — Tuesdays, 3:00pm – 5:00pm
    • Fear and aggression sessions — Wednesdays, 1:00pm – 4:00pm
    • Excessive barking workshops — Fridays, 11:00am – 12:00pm
    • Multi-pet integration — Saturdays, 2:00pm – 4:00pm
    Schedule Consultation

  4. Community Education Programs

    Workshops and seminars on responsible pet ownership, open to adopters and the wider community.

    Hours of Operation:

    • New Dog Owner 101 — First Saturday of each month, 9:00am – 11:00am
    • Understanding Dog Behavior — Second Thursday, 6:00pm – 8:00pm
    • Dog Health and Wellness — Third Wednesday, 7:00pm – 9:00pm
    • Advanced Training Techniques — Last Friday, 5:00pm – 7:00pm
    View Workshops

  5. Veterinary Care Partnerships

    Local veterinarian partnerships that give our adopters access to discounted, trusted medical care.

    Hours of Operation:

    • Discounted initial vet visits — Available year-round at 5 partner clinics
    • Clinic referrals — Monday – Friday, 9:00am – 5:00pm
    • Low-cost spay/neuter — First Tuesday of each month, 8:00am – 2:00pm
    • Emergency care network — 24/7 referrals available
    Find Partner Vet
