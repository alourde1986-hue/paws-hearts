About Max

Max is a wonderful 3-year-old Labrador who came to us after his previous owner could no longer care for him. He’s a friendly, energetic dog who loves people and would make a great family companion.

Max is house-trained, knows basic commands, and gets along well with other dogs. He would do best in an active home where he can get plenty of exercise and mental stimulation.

If you have a yard, that’s a big plus — Max loves to run and play fetch. He’s also great on leash and would happily join you on hikes, runs, or long walks around the neighborhood.
