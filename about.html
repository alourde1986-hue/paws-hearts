About Us

Our Mission

At Paws & Hearts, we rescue, rehabilitate, and find loving homes for abandoned dogs. We believe every dog deserves a second chance at happiness and a family to call their own.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Paws & Hearts began with a simple mission: to give abandoned and neglected dogs a second chance at life. What started as a small group of passionate volunteers has grown into a thriving organization that has saved over 500 dogs and found them forever homes.

Our journey has been filled with heartwarming success stories, from rescuing dogs from difficult situations to watching them thrive in their new families. Every adoption reminds us why we do what we do.
